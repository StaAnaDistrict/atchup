PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy Policy

We, at Sta. Ana Central Elementary School is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have secure access to the school database web application ("SACES ATCHUP School Form 10 Generator") designed to efficiently manage learners' permanent records. This Privacy Policy ("Policy") aims to ensure compliance with applicable privacy laws of the Republic of the Philippines, including Republic Act No. 10173, otherwise known as the Data Privacy Act of 2012 ("Data Privacy Act"), its implementing rules, and the regulations and issuances of the National Privacy Commission of the Philippines ("NPC").

By registering and using the WebApp, you consent to the collection, storage, processing, and sharing of your personal information as described in this Policy. Your continued use of the WebApp indicates your acceptance of the terms herein.

Types of Information Covered by This Policy

This Policy applies to all Personal Information and Sensitive Personal Information ("Personal Data") collected through the WebApp's registration process.

Anonymous, aggregated, or de-identified data that cannot be linked back to an individual is not covered by this Policy.

Scope of This Privacy Policy

This Policy applies to all Personal Information and Sensitive Personal Information ("Personal Data") collected through the WebApp's registration process.

Contact Information

This Policy governs all Personal Data gathered through the WebApp, which is used for managing and accessing learners' permanent records. It applies to school personnel and authorized users accessing the WebApp.

Information Collection

The School collects Personal Information and Sensitive Personal Information through the WebApp for the following purposes:

  • Account Registration: For teachers and school staff to access the WebApp.
  • Learner Records Management: To allow teachers to generate, view, and print learners' permanent records securely.
  • Communication and Support: To respond to inquiries and provide assistance related to WebApp use.

We ensure that only authorized personnel have access to the data collected, and we implement appropriate safeguards to protect Personal Data.

Data Processing Procedure

The School collects and processes Personal Data following strict protocols. Information collected through the WebApp is used to:

  • Operate the system for record management and generation of learner records.
  • Ensure authorized access and maintain the security of the platform.

Only authorized personnel are permitted to access the data, and any unauthorized access will be subject to disciplinary measures and corrective actions. The School monitors and updates its procedures regularly to comply with the Data Privacy Act and other relevant regulations.

Use of Personal Data

The School uses Personal Data to provide efficient and secure access to the WebApp, manage user accounts, and maintain learners' records. Personal Data may also be used for administrative purposes and technical support.

Sharing of Personal Data

The School may share Personal Data with its affiliates, service providers, and partners when necessary to operate and maintain the WebApp. Such sharing will be done under strict confidentiality agreements and compliance with legal requirements. We do not sell or rent your Personal Data to third parties.

Personal Data may also be disclosed without notice if legally required or to protect the School's rights, the safety of users, or the integrity of the WebApp.

How We Safeguard Personal Data

Protecting Personal Data is a top priority. The School has implemented robust security measures, such as encrypted storage, secure user authentication methods, and access controls, to safeguard data collected and processed through the WebApp.

In the event of a data breach involving Personal Data, affected individuals will be notified in accordance with the Data Privacy Act.

Access and Deletion

Users may request to edit, update, or delete their Personal Data from the School's systems by contacting Mr. Wedzmer B. Munjilul at wedzmer.munjilul@deped.gov.ph. The School retains Personal Data for as long as necessary to fulfill legitimate administrative purposes, or as required by law.

If you need to correct or update your Personal Data, please contact us at staana.ces@deped.gov.ph.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The WebApp may use cookies to improve your experience by remembering your preferences and login information. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; however, this may limit some functionalities of the WebApp.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, you may contact our Data Protection Officer at staana.ces@deped.gov.ph.
